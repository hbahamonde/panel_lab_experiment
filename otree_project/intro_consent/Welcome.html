Thank you for considering participation in this study.

This research examines how people evaluate political information over time, how they respond to changing social and political conditions, and how they make decisions in a dynamic campaign environment.

In this study, you will move through a sequence of short campaign stages in a hypothetical society, review political news items, and make decisions based on the information you choose to open. The study is designed for academic research purposes only.

The study is organized in 3 waves:

Wave 1: {{ wave1_date_display }} to {{ wave1_deadline_display }}
Wave 2: {{ wave2_date_display }} to {{ wave2_deadline_display }}
Wave 3: {{ wave3_date_display }} to {{ wave3_deadline_display }}

Each wave remains open for {{ wave_window_days }} days. Please use the same personal study link each time you return to the study.

The results will be used to better understand how citizens process information, update their views, and make political judgments under changing conditions. Findings may be reported in academic publications, presentations, and other scientific outputs.

Your responses will be kept confidential and analyzed only for research purposes. Please read the next page carefully before deciding whether to participate.
