You are invited to participate in a research study about political judgment and decision-making. The study is organized in 3 waves, with approximately one week between waves.

Your participation is voluntary. You may stop participating at any time by closing the study. There is no penalty for withdrawing. However, full compensation is conditional on completing all 3 waves. Each wave lasts about 20 minutes.

Your compensation includes a participation payment and a performance-based component. The performance-based component is common in laboratory experiments and is used to encourage careful attention and sincere decision-making during the study. This means that some of your payment may depend on the choices you make in the tasks. These payments are part of the research design and are not intended to pressure you or to create unreasonable financial risk. The amounts used in the study are limited and proportionate to standard research practice.

Your responses will be used for academic research purposes only. The results of the study may be reported in academic publications, presentations, and other scientific outputs.

Your responses will be kept confidential. No unnecessary personal identifying information will be collected through the study interface.

By agreeing to participate, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old, that you have read this information, and that you consent to take part in the study.
